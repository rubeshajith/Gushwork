Precision Extrusion Process

Molten HDPE compound is forced through a precisely engineered die to form continuous pipe of exact dimensions and consistent wall thickness.

  • Temperature-controlled barrel zones
  • High-output single screw extruder

Controlled Cooling System

The extruded pipe passes through water-filled cooling tanks to rapidly and uniformly solidify the material, locking in dimensional accuracy.

  • Multi-stage water cooling tanks
  • Uniform temperature distribution

Accurate Pipe Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • Vacuum calibration sleeve
  • Real-time diameter monitoring

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes comprehensive inspection including wall thickness measurement, ovality testing, and hydrostatic pressure testing.

  • IS 5984 / ISO 4427 compliance
  • Online ultrasonic wall thickness gauge

Permanent Pipe Marking

Each pipe is permanently marked with product specifications, batch number, pressure rating, and certification details for full traceability.

  • Inkjet printing system
  • Batch and date code traceability

Precision Pipe Cutting

Automated cutting stations deliver burr-free, square-end cuts at preset lengths, ensuring consistent pipe segments ready for installation.

  • Planetary cutting system
  • Auto-length measurement

Safe & Secure Packaging

Finished pipes are bundled, strapped, and labeled for safe transport, preventing damage during handling, shipping, and storage.

  • UV-protected wrapping available
  • Bundle strapping for secure transit
